Science of Light

Light and the
human body.

Light is not merely illumination — it is a biological signal. The human circadian rhythm, our internal 24-hour clock, is governed by the quality, intensity, and colour temperature of the light we experience throughout the day.

Kripton luminaires are engineered with this understanding at their core. From the warm amber of morning to the crisp clarity of midday, our systems adapt to support the natural rhythms of human life.
